New Product success depends on 

* risk (cost and implications for consumers of making a bad choice)
* relative advantage of new innovation
* relative simplicity of new product
* compativility with existing beliefs and behaviours
* ease of product trial
* how easy it is to communicate the benefits

Gate Meetings in the marketting department

* concept birth
* business gate
* prototype and marketing strategy
* operational prototype
* limited test market
* learnings review and product/service refinement
